About Me

I'm Ines Vanessa Namahoro,fashion is both my hobby and my passion. I believe I was born to be a designer, creating all the clothes I wear. You may wonder how, why, and since when. Well, I redesign my clothes by transforming my old ones into new pieces. Previously, I used to spend a lot on buying clothes, only to wear them once or twice before abandoning them. Now, I invest less money in purchasing materials to redesign my existing clothes into new ones, and no one can even tell the difference. My journey began in 2018 when I was in high school. The idea of designing sparked from observing my schoolmates sewing. I asked them to teach me, and that's where my journey started.
